# Twice Baked Potatoes 

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XKiRQG4q_bA&ab_channel=MasonWoodruff

### Air fryer
- 20ish minutes 

### Mix
- parsley
- bacon 
- 2 tablespoons butter
- black pepper
- cheese - lots of cheese

### Scoop potatoes and add
- sour cream
- add mix back into the taters
- back into air fryer with cheese on top 5mins at 400
- top with bacon, chopped chives and sour cream
